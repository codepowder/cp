Present your employees with a 

VIRTUAL GAME ROOM

MOBILE GAMES

The Virtual Game Room (VGR for short) is a suite of mobile games made available to your employees' via their iOS and Android devices, shared and managed internally by our team, on your behalf.

AUGMENTED REALITY

The games are powered by Augmented Reality to create a dynamic and engaging experience, that gets the players moving around. Ideal to get employees away from their desk or station to recharge.

SHARED EXPERIENCE

Employees play with their colleagues in a multiplayer experience, either cooperating or competing against each other individually, or in small teams.

Why play games?

The secret is 
the recovery process 
after doing some work

MENTAL DETACHMENT

While playing a person disengages mentally from negative thoughts arising out of work-related issues that they may have.

RELAXATION

Playing games allows people to return their mental state to baseline levels of psychological and physiological arousal.

MASTERY

The challenging activities that players undertake and overcome, help in building up new competencies and a feeling of self-efficacy.

CONTROL

Games allow people to regain a feeling of control, increasing psychological well-beig by experiencing personal control during leisure time.


Source:
Games at Work: The Recreational Use of Computer Games During Working Hours
Leonard Reinecke, M.Sc.
