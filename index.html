IN EXTENDED REALITY THE DEVICE IS JUST A PANE OF GLASS. THE WORLD WE SEE AROUND US IS OUR INTERFACE.

By 2019, more than a billion AR-enabled devices were in existence.

In 2017, alongside tablet devices, Apple, and Android device manufacturers, began competing to create large edge-to-edge displays on mobile devices, making AR content more immersive. And in 2020, they will be introducing 3D sensing technology to their cameras.

Apple, Google, Microsoft and Magic Leap are close to releasing affordable AR glasses products, with rumours that Apple is to release their first commercial product by 2021.

APPS AND GAMES

Over 3,000 have been published so far for iOS and Android, and the number of new releases and adoption of these apps and games is accelerating.

DOMINANT AR SDKs

Apple ARKit and Google ARCore are seeing rapid adoption ahead of competitors like Vuforia, and are leading the software innovation.

UNITY ENGINE

Unity are leading the way in unifying ARKit and ARCore within a single package, simplifying the development of apps and games using a single code base, which means costs of development are getting lower.
